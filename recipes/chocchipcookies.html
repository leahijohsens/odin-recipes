Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 C flour
  • 3/4 C brown sugar
  • 3/4 C white sugar
  • 1 C unsalted butter
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 C chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Combine butter and sugars, then add egg
  2. Combine all other dry ingredients together
  3. Slowly add the dry to the wet and stir until well combined
  4. Bake at 375Â°F for 8-10 minutes. Let cool on wire rack.
